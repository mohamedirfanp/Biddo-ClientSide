User

Event Management with Reverse Auction is a unique platform that provides users with a hassle-free way of organizing events as per their preferences. Users can easily create events and specify their requirements. Once the auction period begins, users will receive various anonymous bids from vendors, along with their rating. The user can then choose the bid which they find most suitable and comfortable. Additionally, users can communicate with vendors through our platform's chat section, making it easier for them to clarify any queries. With our platform's seamless process, users can plan their events efficiently and effectively.

Vendor

As a vendor, the first step towards participating in an auction is to navigate to the auction section where the relevant auction can be found. Once there, vendors can place their bids for that particular auction and update their bid amount as many times as they want until the auction time ends. This provides a great opportunity for vendors to adjust their bids in response to changes in the auction landscape and stay competitive. Our platform takes great care to keep vendors informed about upcoming auctions so that they can plan accordingly. We send email notifications to all relevant vendors in advance of upcoming auctions, providing them with the necessary details and specifications to ensure that they can make informed decisions when placing their bids. Once a user confirms the bid amount of a particular vendor, the vendor receives a notification about the Bid Confirmation through email. We understand that keeping track of all the auctions that a vendor has participated in can be a challenge. To address this, we provide a dashboard where vendors can view all the events they have participated in. This makes it easy for vendors to monitor their auction history and track their performance over time. Our platform is designed to provide vendors with the tools they need to stay competitive and win bids in a fast-paced and dynamic marketplace.
