Event Management with Reverse Auction

Welcome to our Event Management with Reverse Auction platform! We are your go-to solution for managing your event needs and finding the best vendors at the best prices through our innovative reverse auction system.

User

Event Management with Reverse Auction is a unique platform that provides users with a hassle-free way of organizing events as per their preferences. Users can easily create events and specify their requirements. Once the auction period begins, users will receive various anonymous bids from vendors, along with their rating. The user can then choose the bid which they find most suitable and comfortable.

Vendor

As a vendor, the first step towards participating in an auction is to navigate to the auction section where the relevant auction can be found. Once there, vendors can place their bids for that particular auction and update their bid amount as many times as they want until the auction time ends. This provides a great opportunity for vendors to adjust their bids in response to changes in the auction landscape and stay competitive.
